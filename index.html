Who We Are

Hi there, We Are MaroofSha! We are a microscale handcrafting organization where individual strengths & skills are combined in the pursuit of a common vision. Our team members work as sales persons, marketers, dressmakers, web designers, distributors, retailers, soap makers, chefs, bloggers & photographers. Whatever we manufacture is handmade, from soaps, personal care products to food products.

What we Do

At MaroofSha we handcraft herbal soaps, detergents, skincare products, custom dresses &amop; some food products in micro scale. MaroofSha is a base foundation for MaroofSha.org.

Our Journey

From handmade natural soaps to laundry bars, come along as we journey through the chemistry & artistry of soap making. We use organic herbs & spices as additives & soap colorants. We use both natural essential oils & lab made fragrance oils to scent our soaps depending on the demand. We hope you love our natural & handcrafted soap recipes & enjoy trying them out sometime. Soap making is a part of MaroofSha. All our products are handcrafted with special care, with organic additives from local gardens.
